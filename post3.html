Tokyo 2020 Olympics will not be ‘cancelled’ due to coronavirus, IOC insists

Posted by Muhammad Giri | April 23 2020

The International Olympic Committee insist the cancellation of the Tokyo 2020 Olympics is “not on the agenda”, though they are considering various scenarios amid the coronavirus pandemic.

The IOC has said the Games would always start as planned on 24 July despite the sporting world collectively cancelling the vast majority of events due to covid-19.And President Thomas Bach insists the governing body are still not considering cancellation, despite mounting pressure and concerns from the athletes.
